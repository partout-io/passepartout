Protect your online privacy with Passepartout, a smart VPN client seamlessly integrated with tvOS.

You can use the iOS or macOS app to set up and manage VPN profiles, or upload profiles directly from a web browser over HTTP. iOS and macOS are not required to use Passepartout on Apple TV.

HOW PASSEPARTOUT WORKS ON APPLE TV

The base Apple TV app includes time-limited connections. To remove the time limit and unlock the full Apple TV experience, an in-app purchase is required.
